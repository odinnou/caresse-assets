Frequently asked questions

What you want to know

Does Caresse really work when you know each other very well?

That's exactly what it's designed for. Knowing each other well lets you configure precisely what attracts you. The AI builds on that foundation to generate something unexpected.

How to use Caresse together without headphones?

Place the phone on a nearby surface, use the speaker or connect to a bluetooth speaker. The voice guides both partners naturally, without anyone having to hold the device.

What if one of us is less enthusiastic than the other?

That's often the starting situation. Configuring together and letting the voice take over allows the hesitant one to be carried along without having to "make an effort." Immersion builds naturally.

Does the content get repetitive over time?

No, that's structurally impossible. Each session is generated on the fly by the AI, even with the same preferences as input. You can use Caresse every week for years without ever hearing the same session.

Can Caresse help get through a dry spell in the relationship?

Yes, and that's one of its most common uses. By removing the pressure of initiative and creating a neutral framework, Caresse allows you to reconnect naturally, without it feeling like a "couple effort."

How often should long-term couples use Caresse?

There's no rule. Some couples use it as a monthly ritual, others during special moments like a romantic weekend. The important thing is that it remains a choice, not an obligation.
