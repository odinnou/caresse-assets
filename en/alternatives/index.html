Three structural differentiators. (1) Audio demos without account creation, in English, French and Spanish — competitors require sign-up to discover. Caresse is pay-per-session, not subscription. Depending on how often you use it, it's often cheaper: you don't pay a yearly subscription for a service you'd use 4 or 5 times a year. Caresse stands out by being the only one with no-account demos, a unique scenario every session with your names, and pay-per-session.
