Dipsea

The US benchmark for erotic audio stories, subscription-based (~$69.99/yr, observed June 2026). Very large catalog, recognized audio production quality. Guided sexual wellness audio

Ferly

English-language app that blends guided audios, meditations and educational content around sexual wellness. Highly educational and inclusive approach, individual-leaning. Catalog of pre-recorded experiences, subscription model.

Erotic audio stories

Bloom Stories

Short English audio stories on subscription. Catalog updated regularly. Use case close to Dipsea: solo or couple listening to pre-recorded stories. No deep personalization, no names spoken aloud.

Caresse. Dozens of real audio demos in English, French and Spanish, playable immediately — no sign-up, no email, no account. The whole app runs anonymously, payments included. That's a structural difference from almost every competitor, which requires sign-up upfront.

What makes Caresse unique among these alternatives?

Three structural differentiators. (1) Audio demos without account creation, in English, French and Spanish — competitors require sign-up to discover. (2) 100% anonymous use, payments included — no other app in this segment offers it. (3) Scenario generated uniquely for the two of you every session, with your names, precisely configured (duration, intensity, practices, setting, atmosphere) — every other app runs on a pre-recorded experiences catalog.

Is Caresse cheaper than competing subscriptions?

Caresse is pay-per-session, not subscription. Depending on how often you use it, it's often cheaper: you don't pay a yearly subscription for a service you'd use 4 or 5 times a year. Most competitors run on subscriptions (Coelle ~$49.99/year, Dipsea ~$69.99/year, InTheMoment ~£7.99/month on Pro, all observed June 2026).

Which alternative should I pick if I want English content?

Most alternatives are English-first: Coelle, Dipsea, Ferly, Bloom Stories, InTheMoment. Caresse runs native English (and French, and Spanish) within the same app, and you can listen to demos before any sign-up. Dipsea has the largest English catalog of erotic audio stories, Coelle is the strongest on guided intimacy. Caresse stands out by being the only one with no-account demos, a unique scenario every session with your names, and pay-per-session.

Is there a pay-per-session alternative to Caresse?

To our knowledge, Caresse is the only app in this segment that offers pay-per-session. All known alternatives — Melba, Coelle, Dipsea, Ferly, Bloom Stories, InTheMoment — work on a subscription model.
