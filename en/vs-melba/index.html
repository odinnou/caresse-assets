In one sentence

The honest summary.

The details

Two approaches, two philosophies.

Melba chose curation. Caresse chose generation. The promise isn't the same.

Caresse

Listen first. Decide later.

Before any purchase, you get access to dozens of real audio demos in English, French and Spanish. No sign-up, no account, no email: you hear right away the sound quality, the voice, the atmosphere and the intensity levels. The demos don't speak your name or use your profile, but they faithfully reflect what you're about to experience.

When you start a bespoke session, Caresse builds a scenario from your profile: who you are, what you go by, what you want tonight, the time you have, the intensity, the atmosphere. The voice names you. The scenario doesn't look like any other, because it was only ever written for this session. And the entire app runs anonymously — payments included.

  • Dozens of audio demos playable without an account (EN · FR · ES)
  • 100% anonymous use — no account creation, even to pay
  • Names spoken aloud in the narration
  • Precise duration (you choose the time you have)
  • Essentially infinite combinations: genders, practices, accessories, intensity, setting, atmosphere
  • Native couple and solo modes
  • No subscription: you pay per session

Melba

A French-language library curated by therapists.

Melba advertises dozens of pre-recorded audio experiences, designed with couple therapists, and a catalog that's updated regularly. The promise is that of a therapeutic companion: discovery programs, expert articles, interactive challenges. Audio is played as-is for every user.

A good fit if you're looking for a reassuring frame, professional validation, and don't mind replaying. Less of a fit if you want to listen without signing up first, if you want the voice to call you by name, or if you want a language other than French.

  • Catalog of pre-recorded experiences
  • Content validated by couple therapists
  • Discovery programs and editorial articles
  • Available in French
  • Subscription model, account required

Frequently asked questions

Caresse vs Melba — your questions.

Can I listen to Caresse before paying, without creating an account?

Yes. Caresse offers dozens of real audio demos in English, French and Spanish, playable immediately — no sign-up, no email, no account. You hear the sound quality, the voice, the atmosphere and the intensity levels before any decision. Demos don't use your name or profile, but they faithfully reflect the experience.

Does Caresse require an account like most competitors?

No. Caresse is fully anonymous: no account creation is ever required to use the app. Even purchases happen without an account. That's a structural difference from almost every competitor, where signing up is a prerequisite for everything.

What's the main difference between Caresse and Melba?

Melba offers a catalog of pre-recorded audio experiences in French, designed with therapists and listened to as-is by every user. Caresse generates a unique scenario for the two of you, in English, French or Spanish: your names are spoken aloud, duration is precise, and intensity, practices and atmosphere are configured every time. No two Caresse sessions are ever the same, and the whole app runs anonymously.

Does Caresse run on a subscription or pay-per-session?

Caresse is pay-per-session — no mandatory monthly subscription. You pay when you want a new experience, you don't pay for months you don't use it. Before any purchase, free audio demos let you decide.

Is Caresse content as polished as Melba's?

Yes. Every Caresse session runs through a multi-phase pipeline (setup, immersion, first touches, exploration, intensity crescendo, polishing) where every prompt is written and refined by the Caresse editorial team. The voice is then synthesized with premium TTS engines. The result is coherent, rhythmically paced, and never generic. The free audio demos let you judge for yourself before paying.

How many different scenarios can Caresse generate?

The combinatorial space is essentially infinite. Each intimate profile combines participants' genders, names, practices, accessories, intensity, dirty-talk level, setting, duration and atmosphere. Where Melba offers dozens of fixed experiences, Caresse generates a new, unique one every time.
