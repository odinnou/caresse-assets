Melt generates erotic text. Caresse generates audio. Same personalization philosophy, radically different medium.

No sign-up, no account, no email: you immediately hear the sound quality, the voice grain, the atmosphere and the intensity levels. Melt Stories

Personalized erotic text, no account needed.

Melt Stories positions itself as a tool for couples who feel a desire gap, with a focus on emotional and intimate reconnection. The platform offers guided stories accessible via an account.

It's a good option if you're looking for a narrative framework to restart the conversation and intimacy as a couple, without configuration each session. It's less suited if you want to listen without creating an account, have the voice speak your names, or need audio in French or Spanish.

Frequently asked questions

Do Caresse and Melt require an account?

Neither requires an account. Melt works directly without sign-up. Caresse also works without an account, including for payments — and offers dozens of free audio demos so you can hear the quality before buying anything.

What is the real difference between Caresse and Melt?

The medium. Melt generates erotic text you read. Caresse generates audio you listen to — a synthesized voice speaks your names and narrates a 25–35 min scenario. Same AI personalization logic, radically different experience.

Does Caresse offer content in French, unlike Melt?

Yes. Caresse generates audio in French, English and Spanish. Melt is available in English only. If you're looking for a personalized intimate experience in French or Spanish, Caresse is the only option in this segment.

How much does Caresse cost?

Caresse displays its prices clearly: €2.99 for 1 audio experience of 25–35 min, €8.49 for 3, €11.99 for 5. Each generated experience is available for life. Melt does not publicly communicate its pricing model.

Can you really compare Caresse and Melt?

Not directly — they are different mediums. Melt makes you read a generated erotic story. Caresse makes you listen to a scenario narrated by a voice that speaks your name. Eyes-closed, screen-free vs literary reading experience. Both AI-personalized, both without a mandatory account. The free Caresse audio demos let you hear the difference before buying.

How many different scenarios can Caresse generate?

The combinations are virtually infinite. Each profile combines participants' genders, names, practices, accessories, intensity level, dirty-talk level, location, duration and atmosphere. You never listen to the same thing twice.
