Why it changes everything

AI without a curated prompt is fine. With one, it's right.

On intimate ground, the difference between "fine" and "right" is everything.

The 6-phase Caresse pipeline

A Caresse session isn't generated in one shot. It runs through six successive phases, each with its dedicated prompt, written for its role in the narrative arc: setup, immerse, open, explore, crescendo, polish. Each prompt is versioned, tested, refined, with specific variants for couple mode and solo mode.

Why this much care? Because on intimate ground, a generic prompt produces flat narration, without rhythm, that pulls you out of the scene instead of pulling you in. The Caresse team spent months tuning the warmth of the voice, the cadence, the exact moment intensity rises. You see none of this machinery — you just live a session that sounds right.

Frequently asked questions

Caresse vs InTheMoment — your questions.

Can I listen to Caresse before paying, without creating an account?

Yes. Caresse offers dozens of real audio demos in English, French and Spanish, playable immediately — no sign-up, no email, no account. The whole app runs anonymously, payments included. That's a major difference from InTheMoment and almost every competitor that requires account creation.

Are Caresse and InTheMoment the same use case?

No. InTheMoment is an AI-personalized meditation and hypnosis app. Caresse is a guided intimate audio app for couples and solo exploration. This page doesn't compare use cases — it compares the AI approach of the two apps, because that's where we're legitimately positioned to weigh in.

Why does Caresse talk about "curated prompts"?

Because AI generation without editorial direction produces content that's fine but generic. Every Caresse session runs through a multi-phase pipeline — setup, immersion, first touches, exploration, intensity crescendo, polishing — whose prompts are written, tested and refined by the Caresse team. Those prompts guarantee pacing, warmth of voice, narrative coherence. Same difference as text produced raw by AI vs text produced by an expert who knows how to drive the AI.

Does Caresse personalize as much as InTheMoment?

Caresse personalizes more on its intimate dimension: names spoken aloud, precise duration, participants' genders, practices, accessories, intensity, dirty-talk level, setting, atmosphere. InTheMoment personalizes on its meditation/hypnosis dimension: voice, technique, posture, length, language (9 languages on Pro).

Does Caresse work on a subscription like InTheMoment?

No, Caresse is pay-per-session. InTheMoment offers a free plan (2 sessions/day) and a Pro plan at around £7.99/month (as of Q1 2026). Caresse has no monthly subscription: you pay for the session when you want to live it.

Is Caresse available in multiple languages like InTheMoment?

Caresse is available natively in English, French and Spanish. InTheMoment covers 9 languages on its Pro plan (German, French, Italian, Spanish, Arabic, Polish, Dutch, etc.) — broader, but for a different use case (meditation, not intimate).
