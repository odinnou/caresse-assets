Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know

How do you listen to Caresse together in the same room?

Caresse is designed to be experienced together in the same room. Use the speaker or connect to a bluetooth speaker for more presence and shared immersion.

Is the content explicit?

Caresse offers varying intensity levels, from gentle romantic to more explicit. You choose exactly what suits you — no visual content in any case. The experience relies entirely on audio and your imagination.

Are our data and preferences shared?

Your profiles and preferences remain strictly private. Caresse does not share any sensitive data — neither with third parties nor between users. An alias is enough to create your profile.

Can Caresse really reignite connection in a long-term couple?

Yes — one of the most commonly reported effects is the surprise it creates. The AI generates experiences you wouldn't have thought to suggest yourselves, creating novelty without effort or negotiation.

Do you need to be on the same phone?

No — each partner uses their own device. Caresse is available on iOS and Android. Configure the session together, then each launch it on your phone.

What if one of us is shy or uncomfortable with guided intimacy?

Caresse always starts gently and never pushes beyond what you've chosen. Configuring together is already a first step — you define boundaries before the session even begins.
