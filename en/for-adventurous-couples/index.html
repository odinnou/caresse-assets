Frequently asked questions

What you want to know

Is the explicit content really unfiltered?

Yes — at high intensity levels, Caresse generates direct adult content without metaphors or euphemisms. You enable this level deliberately in configuration; nothing arrives by default.

Can we explore dominance and submission dynamics?

Yes. D/s dynamics, power play, and guided roleplay are configurable options. You define who takes which role, and the voice builds the experience around that, with matching narrative tension.

How does Caresse handle specific fantasies?

During setup, you can indicate themes, worlds, or scenarios you want to explore. The AI integrates them into the generated session. You're not proposing the fantasy directly to each other — the app receives it.

Are there contents Caresse refuses to generate?

Yes. All illegal content is blocked, no exceptions, no configuration possible. Caresse is made for pleasure between consenting adults, and that doesn't change.

How do we stop if one of us is no longer comfortable mid-session?

You can pause or stop the session at any time from the app. You remain fully in control throughout. Configuring together beforehand is also designed to minimize this risk.

Are our preferences and fantasies stored anywhere?

Caresse does not store sensitive data linked to your content or intimate preferences in an identifiable way. An alias is enough to create your profile. Your configurations stay between you and the app.
