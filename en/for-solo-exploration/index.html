Frequently asked questions

What you want to know

Can Caresse really be used alone?

Absolutely. Solo mode is a pillar of Caresse, just as complete and customizable as duo mode. The experience is designed to be fulfilling solo — not a "reduced" version of couple mode.

Do my preferences remain private?

Yes — your preferences and profile are never shared or used for commercial purposes. No third party has access to your session history. The experience remains strictly between you and the app.

Does the content adapt to my tastes?

Every session is generated to measure based on your current preferences — atmosphere, tone, intensity, practices. You're never locked into a fixed profile: you can change everything with each session.

Can solo exploration help me know myself better?

This is often the most unexpected effect. By exploring different configurations without pressure or outside gaze, many users discover preferences they wouldn't have identified otherwise.

How long is a solo session?

Duration varies based on your preferences — generally between 15 and 40 minutes. You can stop at any time, resume later, or launch a new session immediately.

Can I use Caresse even if I'm in a relationship?

Yes — solo mode is independent from duo mode. Many users in relationships use Caresse individually to explore alone, then share the experience in duo mode at other times.
