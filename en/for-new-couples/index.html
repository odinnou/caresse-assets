Frequently asked questions

What you want to know

Is Caresse suitable for very new relationships?

Yes — Caresse is designed for all relationship stages. Intensity levels can start very gently, from romantic to more explicit. You choose.

How does Caresse help talk about desires?

The session setup is a list of preferences to explore together — atmosphere, practices, dynamics. It's a conversation guided by options, not words to find alone.

What if we disagree on some preferences?

You always configure what works for both partners — if an option doesn't appeal, simply don't select it. Consent is at the heart of the experience.

Is Caresse suitable for varied orientations and dynamics?

Yes — Caresse is designed to be inclusive. All orientations, gender identities, and relationship dynamics are welcome. The setup lets you define exactly who you are and what draws you in.

Can Caresse be used on a first date?

Caresse is better suited for people who already know each other a bit — configuring together involves talking about preferences. It's ideal from the moment you're comfortable having that conversation.

What if we don't agree on intensity?

This is often where Caresse helps most. The setup naturally creates a conversation about your respective boundaries — without pressure or confrontation. You always choose the option that works for both.
